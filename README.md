Pharmacy Calculator Suite

Pharmacy workflow calculator suite focused on operational math, day supply logic, and refill timing.

Built to reduce repetitive pharmacy calculations while keeping human oversight and pharmacist judgment front-and-center.

⸻

Purpose

Most online calculators can count days or divide numbers.

They do not understand pharmacy workflow logic.

This suite was built around real operational pain points including:

* refill-too-soon calculations
* insulin day supply
* ophthalmic drop calculations
* nasal spray usage
* inhaler actuations
* enzyme dosing
* refrigerated shipment timing
* titration scheduling

The goal is simple:

Reduce arithmetic friction without replacing clinical judgment.

Design Philosophy

This project intentionally avoids:

* automated clinical decision-making
* prescribing logic
* insurance adjudication
* EHR integration
* AI recommendations
* patient-specific automation

This is a workflow support tool.

Nothing more.

Nothing less.

Every calculation should still be reviewed by the user.

⸻

Key Principles

Manual-First

The calculator assists workflow.

It does not replace pharmacist verification or policy review.
