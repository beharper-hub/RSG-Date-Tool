Workflow Support Only

Pharmacy Calc Suite is intended for arithmetic and workflow support only. It is not medical advice, clinical decision support, prescribing guidance, billing guidance, legal advice, or a substitute for professional judgment.
Always verify results against the current prescription, prescriber directions, pharmacy system adjudication, package labeling, payer requirements, current workplace policy, and pharmacist guidance.

No Clinical Judgment Replacement

This tool does not determine whether a medication, dose, refill, shipment, or claim is clinically appropriate. It does not evaluate patient-specific medical conditions, allergies, labs, diagnosis, renal/hepatic function, drug interactions, contraindications, or therapeutic appropriateness.

Any clinical concern, unclear direction, unusual dose, questionable day supply, controlled-substance concern, shipping concern, or policy exception should be escalated according to applicable pharmacy workflow and pharmacist direction.

No PHI Storage or Transmission

This app is designed to perform calculations in the browser. Do not enter protected health information, patient identifiers, full names, dates of birth, medical record numbers, insurance identifiers, addresses, phone numbers, or other patient-identifying information.

The app does not require accounts, logins, cloud sync, or server-side patient records. Browser features such as local storage, cache, history, screenshots, autofill, device backups, or shared devices may still preserve information outside the app's control. Use appropriate workplace privacy practices.

Calculation Limits

  • Results are estimates based on the values entered by the user.
  • Incorrect inputs produce incorrect outputs. Garbage in, garbage out — computers are loyal, not smart.
  • Rounding rules, payer requirements, plan limits, package sizes, quantity limits, and workplace policies may differ from calculator assumptions.
  • Holiday, delivery, and shipping checks may not include every carrier exception, weather delay, local delay, patient-specific restriction, or operational change.
  • Medication packaging, priming waste, drop factors, device limits, and titration schedules should be verified against current product and workflow guidance.

User Responsibility

By using this app, the user accepts responsibility for independently verifying all calculations and applying current pharmacy policy, pharmacist guidance, and professional standards before acting on any result.
